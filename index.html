The Architects of Purity

Manoj &
Bharat Jain.

What began as a shared vision between two brothers has transformed into one of India’s most trusted wholesale bullion institutions. Founded in 1984, Ambaa Gold was built on the simple yet radical principle that in the precious metals trade,

"Integrity is the only currency that matters."

Manoj and Bharat Jain combined traditional craftsmanship with an uncompromising approach to transparency. They understood that to serve the wholesale market, they needed to be more than just suppliers; they needed to be guardians of their partners' reputations.
